# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The GoTestX community is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and respectful environment for everyone.

We welcome contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels. Our goal is to foster an open and collaborative
community around Go tooling and developer productivity.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this project are expected to:

* Be respectful and considerate toward others
* Provide constructive feedback
* Accept differing opinions and viewpoints
* Focus on improving the project and community
* Communicate professionally in issues, pull requests, and discussions

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

* Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
* Discrimination of any kind
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
* Publishing private information without consent
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove or edit comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate these guidelines.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from participating in the project.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainer.

* Maintainer: Isidro A. López G.
* Official repository: https://github.com/entiqon/gotestx

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.
